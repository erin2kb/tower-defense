Story

The students tease each other about their various bug-related phobias. Later that night, they find themselves trapped in a shared dream world. If they ever hope to wake up again, they must manipulate positive dream energy and fight their fears to prevent the dream from becoming a permanent nightmare.



Gameplay

This game is strongly influenced by Plants vs. Zombies, and my goal is to give it a similar look and feel. It is 2D, the tower building is grid-based, and the player must protect the left side of the screen against the enemies who approach from the right.

The player stores up a resource called "dream energy" and spends it to build towers called "dream crystals", which shoot lasers at enemies and also slowly generate dream energy.

Enemies are manifestations of "nightmare energy", in the form of giant bugs. They will single-mindedly advance, converting the dream into a nightmare as they progress.

In the dream world, the heroes possess armor and weapons. This enables them to move around the battlefield and assist their towers by attacking enemies themselves.
