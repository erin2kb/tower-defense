Among the titular thousand, these bugs are currently giving us a hard time...


Issue 1: [INTERMITTENT] Enemy health bars don't always work
Priority: High (health bars should always work)
Discovered by: Erin

I noticed this issue a couple of times while I was playing with the initial enemy spawn rate. I had a bunch of towers firing, but the enemy didn't seem to be taking any damage. Unfortunately, I can't remember if it was actually not dying, or if it was just that the health bar wasn't working. In either case, I haven't come up with a way to reliably reproduce this, and I'm starting to wonder if it might have been an illusion created by Issue 2...



Issue 2: Current target can become hidden beneath other enemies
Priority: High (it may also fix issue 1)
Discovered by: Erin

If a hero is blocking two or more enemies while attacking, the enemy taking the attacks is usually completely obscured by the other enemies standing in the same spot. Ideally, the one we're attacking should be the one that we see. This problem may also affect towers firing at multiple overlapping enemies, which could explain Issue 1.

Issue 3: [INTERMITTENT] Enemy attack animations not always used when enemy is attacking a tower
Priority: Moderate (affects UX but not gameplay)
Discovered by: Erin

I feel like this tends to happen to me when I have my hero blocking/attacking an enemy close to a tower, then I move my hero and the enemy proceeds to the tower. Or I could just be crazy; requires further investigation.



Issue 4: [INTERMITTENT] The killing shot sometimes causes "negative" areas of the health bar to draw
Priority: Low (affects UX but not gameplay; only visible for a second)
Discovered by: Erin

Occasionally, the health bar will show a red portion outside its leftmost border as an enemy dies. This is clearly due to a negative value being used in the drawing process; I need to ensure such negatives do not reach the drawing function.



Issue 5: Pre-emptive strikes don't seem to work
Priority: High (affects gameplay)
Discovered by: Erin

I like to line up my hero with an approaching enemy and begin my attack before they reach me, with timing such that they step into my attack as they're trying to reach me. However, I have not yet been successful with this maneuver. It's possible that my timing and hand-eye coordination are just that terrible, but given that it really looks like my spear hits the enemy, I expect this is actually a bug. Bounding box collision detection should occur at the peak of the strike, not when the attack animation begins.



Issue 6: [INTERMITTENT] On occasion, multiple enemies will spawn at the start of the level
Priority: Moderate (makes level harder, but not impossible)
Discovered by: Erin

There should only be one enemy at the very start of the level, but on rare occasion, I've seen two. I wonder if I'm handling the randomness of the first spawn timer correctly; maybe it ended up set to zero or some other small number.
