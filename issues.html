Among the thousand, these bugs are currently giving us a hard time...


Issue 1: Sometimes enemy health bars don't seem to work
Discovered by: Erin

I noticed this issue a couple of times while I was playing with the initial enemy spawn rate. I had a bunch of towers firing, but the enemy didn't seem to be taking any damage. Unfortunately, I can't remember if it was actually not dying, or if it was just that the health bar wasn't working. In either case, I haven't come up with a way to reliably reproduce this, and I'm starting to wonder if it might have been an illusion created by Issue 2...



Issue 2: Current target can become hidden beneath other enemies
Discovered by: Erin

Arguably this is more of a feature request than a bug, but if a hero is blocking two or more enemies while attacking, the enemy taking the attacks is usually completely obscured by the other enemies standing in the same spot. Ideally, the one we're attacking should be the one that we see. This problem may also affect towers firing at multiple overlapping enemies, which could explain Issue 1.
